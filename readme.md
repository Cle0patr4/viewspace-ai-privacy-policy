# Privacy Policy

**ViewSpace AI**
**Last Updated: February 21, 2026**

## Introduction

ViewSpace AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates a Shopify application that enables furniture store customers to visualize products in their own spaces using AI-powered image processing. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application.

This policy applies to both **merchants** (Shopify store owners who install our app) and **end users** (customers who use the "View in My Space" feature on merchant stores).

## Information We Collect

### From Merchants

When you install ViewSpace AI on your Shopify store, we collect:

- **Store information**: Store name, domain, and Shopify store ID, provided through the Shopify OAuth authentication process.
- **Product data**: Product names, descriptions, and images necessary to provide the visualization feature.
- **Account information**: Email address for communication regarding your account and support.

### From End Users

When customers use the "View in My Space" feature, we collect:

- **Room photographs**: Images uploaded by users of their spaces for the purpose of product visualization. These images are processed by our AI to generate composite images showing products in the user's space.
- **Generated images**: The composite images created by our AI placing products in the user's space.
- **Device information**: Basic device type and browser information necessary to deliver the service.

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

- Provide the product visualization service.
- Process room photographs and generate composite images with AI.
- Display visualization results to end users.
- Communicate with merchants about their account, updates, and support.
- Improve and optimize our service.

## Data Retention

- **Room photographs**: Uploaded images are processed and retained for up to 30 days to enable the visualization experience, after which they are automatically deleted.
- **Generated images**: Composite visualization images are retained for up to 30 days and then automatically deleted.
- **Merchant data**: Store and product information is retained for as long as the app remains installed. Upon uninstallation, merchant data is deleted within 30 days.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties, except:

- **AI processing providers**: Room photographs are sent to our AI processing infrastructure to generate product visualizations. These providers process data solely on our behalf and are bound by data processing agreements.
- **Hosting providers**: We use secure cloud infrastructure to store and process data.
- **Legal requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure cloud storage, and access controls.

## Your Rights

### For Merchants

You may request to access, correct, or delete your data at any time by contacting us or by uninstalling the app from your Shopify store.

### For End Users

- You may choose not to use the "View in My Space" feature.
- Room photographs are used only for the visualization you request.
- You may contact us to request deletion of any data associated with your use of the service.

## GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process personal data based on the following legal bases:

- **Consent**: By uploading a photograph, end users consent to its processing for visualization purposes.
- **Legitimate interest**: For merchants, processing is necessary to provide the contracted service.

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and port your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify merchants of material changes via email or through the Shopify app dashboard.
